The Personal Page

Hi there, My names Michaela Tuor.
I was born and raised in Canada's Capital City, Ottawa Ontario. Travelling is something that I love and I hope to get out and explore the world someday. That being said for now travelling to my cottage every free moment I have will have to do! It is where I get to do one of my all time favorite pass times; Getting lost withing the world of books. Furthermore, with all the nature surrounding me it is a great place to pull inspriation out of for my designs. Competive Curling is what I grewing up knowing and has been an active part of my life since before I can remember. It's a great sport that really challenges you, and I wouldnt be who I am today without it. I graduated merivale high school from the Communications and Design Focus Program. I knew after that, that Graphic Design was my passion and enrolled into Algonquin Colleges Graphic Design Program where I have been studying for going on three years in a hopes to someday work as a graphic designer .

Why I am a Designer
I decided to become a designer at first because it was what I was good at. Slowly the more I learn and grow as a designer its become more of a passion and now I hope to make a difference and help people with design.
